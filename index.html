                    About Quality OverStock

                    Quality Overstock has been in the wholesale and retail trade of crystal & collectibles since the 1980s. Throughout the years we have consistently surpassed customers' expectations with our outstanding selection, prices, and service. Since our inception, our aim has been to earn the trust and satisfaction of our customers. We therefore combine competitive prices with honest, helpful dealings and superior service before, during, and after each sale. Indeed, our success is reflected in the long-standing relationships we have built with our customers.

                    Quality Overstock's product lines consist of Swarovski, Lladro, Lalique, Daum, Baccarat, Armani and selected others. We can orders items upon request as long as they haven't been discontinued or retired. To ensure your comfort, security, and confidence, we offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee on all transactions. Furthermore, all transactions are digitally encrypted using the latest technologies, and we never share your information with anyone. Orders are processed efficiently and shipped securely. Whether you're shopping with us for the first time, or have bought from us repeatedly, we are determined to simplify the process to one of complete satisfaction.

                    Return Policy

                    Satisfaction guaranteed or your money back!
                    Quality Overstock's goal is that you be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not satisfied, simply return your purchase in its original packaging along with your original packing slip, within 7 days of receipt, for a refund or exchange. We'll also pay the return shipping costs if the return is a result of our error (i.e. incorrect or defective item). If you are not satisfied with your purchase after this time period, please let us know. Your complete satisfaction is of most importance to us.

                    QualityOverstock.com reserves the right to reject returns sent or communicated beyond the return period limit, or items which are not in the same condition in which they were received. Returns are accepted within seven days of receipt; items must be in transit within that time period.

                    Shipping Policy

                    ALL IN STOCK ITEMS WILL SHIP WITHIN 24-48 HOURS (certain exclusions apply). WE OFFER A SHIPPING DISCOUNT FOR MULTIPLE PURCHASES.

                    DOMESTIC BUYERS: All items are carefully and securely packaged for safe transport and are fully insured. You will receive an automated email with the Tracking Number the day your package is being shipped, which is typically within 1-2 days after the close of the purchase. (If you do not receive the email it may be due to your Junk Mail filter.)

                    INTERNATIONAL BUYERS: The following USPS shipping methods are available: First Class Airmail - approx. 2-3 weeks (NO TRACKING SERVICES AVAILABLE). Priority Airmail - 2 weeks with tracking services. Express - 3-5 bus. days with tracking services.

                    Please bear in mind that packages sent overseas are sometimes delayed because of Customs and may take longer to arrive than specified by USPS.
